PAYMENTS METHODS:

                A Payment method is a way that customers pay for a product or service.

                Accepted payment methods may include cash on delivery,gift card,credit card,prepaid card,mobile payments
                and net banking.

                The technical chioce is vital in securing reliable and unproblematic cash flow.

                We can accept several payment methods both online and in offline.Each has benefits and its downside.

                The first most obvious opportunity is payment in cash.This is relatively simple for both customers and
                sellers.

ADVANTAGES:

                *Increased speed and convenience.
                *Increased sales.
                *Reduced transaction costs.
